St. Vincent Church Hang Hau

Announcements

Last updated: XX-XX-2014

  1. Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion
    There will be a meeting today after 10:30 Mass for all those who serve or wish to serve as extraordinary ministers for the distribution of the Holy Sacrament.
  2. Special Adult Catechesis
    The first meeting of the special catechesis for those adults over 18 who have not yet received the sacrament of confirmation will be today after 10:30 Mass. 
  3. Holy Week Conference
    Fr. Lam will be holding conferences on the significance of Holy Week this afternoon and next Sunday, March 2, at 2 pm at St. Vincent’s. All are invited to attend; each meeting will explicate a different part of Holy Week.
  4. Adoration of the Holy Sacrament
    All are invited to attend the Adoration of the Holy Sacrament today at 3:30 pm here at St. Vincent’s. We will pray particularly for the persecuted Churches throughout the world, and especially in the Middle East and in China; and for peace in Africa and the Ukraine.
  5. “The Good Life of 290,000 People
    In light of widespread public concern over the recent incidents of abuse suffered by foreign domestic helpers, the Justice and Peace Commission of the Hong Kong Catholic Diocese is organizing a dialogue session in Cantonese and English this afternoon at 2:30 pm in the parish hall of St. Andrew’s Church in Hang Hau. Parishioners, especially employers of foreign domestic helpers, are invited to join in hopes of encouraging mutual understanding.
  6. Pancake Day
    Pancake Day is celebrated in many countries on Shrove Tuesday, the day before the beginning of Lent. Next Sunday after 10:30 Mass we will have pancake races and hot pancakes for sale. Money raised will go to the church. There will be NO PARKING by the downstairs church hall that day. Please speak to Terry Gamao after 10:30 Mass if you can help out with this enjoyable event.
